UNDERSTANDING

Structure Of The Course

Six chapters comprise 19 videos consisting of both lectures and exercises. Each chapter deals with a specific aspect of anxiety and builds on the previous chapters, developing your skills to address specific anxiety symptoms and causes.

We recommend allowing two to three weeks between each chapter, providing sufficient time to trial each exercise and record the successful strategies in your personal workbook provided with the course.

As you build your personalized therapy program we provide ongoing assistance via email support should you have queries. By the time you complete the course you will have your own customised, proven, therapy system that you can return to whenever life places a hurdle in front of you.

Additionally, your access to the course is not time limited and you can return at any time to refresh your understanding of strategies or techniques.

STRUCTURE

Simple, Progressive and Powerful

No two people “do” anxiety the same way. Therefore no one therapy technique works for everyone. The course initially helps you to understand YOUR anxiety better by guided questioning. Each chapter then builds on the previous moving to the management of your anxiety on a day to day basis; controlling and reducing symptoms using a broad range of proven techniques. You’ll then attack the anxiety itself, using various techniques to re-condition your responses to trigger events and develop the new confident you.

Finally, you’ll improve your sleep patterns and banish insomnia. At the conclusion of the course, you should not only see a dramatic improvement in your life, you’ll have a blueprint of treatment that you can use forevermore. Time to retire your therapist!
